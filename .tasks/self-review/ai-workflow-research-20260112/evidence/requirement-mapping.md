## Key Findings Summary

1. **Trend**: Industry moving from "vibe coding" to structured spec-driven development
2. **Pattern**: Specify → Plan → Tasks → Implement workflow becoming standard
3. **Technology**: Multi-agent orchestration gaining traction
4. **Best Practice**: Human-in-the-loop at key decision points
